## 👩‍💼 About Me

</div>

> *"I don't just tick compliance boxes — I build the frameworks that protect people, data, and institutions."*

Hello! I'm **Yasaswini Thuraka**, a dedicated **Cybersecurity Compliance Consultant** based in **Milton Keynes, UK**, with **7 years of hands-on experience** safeguarding Tier-1 UK financial institutions against evolving regulatory and cyber threats.

My journey into cybersecurity compliance began with a deep-rooted passion for governance, risk management, and protecting critical infrastructure. Over the years, I have grown into a trusted advisor who bridges the gap between complex regulatory requirements and practical, business-aligned security controls.

Currently, I serve as a **Compliance Consultant at Lloyds Banking Group**, where I own and lead the ISO 27001 Information Security Management System — covering **500+ security controls**, driving audit readiness, and embedding compliance into the DNA of one of the UK's largest financial institutions. Prior to this, I spent over 3 years at **Barclays** as a Cyber Security Analyst, delivering security monitoring, vulnerability management, and regulatory compliance programmes.

**What sets me apart:**

- 🏛️ **Deep Regulatory Expertise:** I navigate the full spectrum of UK & international frameworks — ISO 27001, GDPR, PCI-DSS, NIST CSF, SOX, DDA — translating them into clear, actionable policies and controls
- 🔍 **Audit-Ready Mindset:** Experienced in leading internal and external audits, producing board-level technical reports, and maintaining continuous controls monitoring programmes
- 🤝 **Stakeholder Collaboration:** I work across legal, IT, risk, and senior leadership teams to embed a culture of security and compliance at every level
- 📊 **Data-Driven Compliance:** I leverage KPI dashboards and compliance metrics to provide real-time visibility of risk posture to executive leadership
- 🌐 **UK Financial Services Specialist:** My expertise is specifically tailored to the FCA-regulated environment, including requirements unique to Lloyds Banking Group and the broader UK financial sector
